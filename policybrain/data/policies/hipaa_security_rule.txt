Title: HIPAA Security Rule
Section: 164.308 Administrative Safeguards

Covered entities must ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability
of all electronic protected health information (ePHI) that they create, receive,
maintain, or transmit.

Covered entities may use cloud computing services provided that the cloud service
provider signs a Business Associate Agreement and implements appropriate
administrative, physical, and technical safeguards.

Failure to implement required safeguards may result in noncompliance penalties.
